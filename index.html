Projects

vgCal

Find it faster.

Finding release dates for games is harder than it needs to be.

The easiest thing to do is Google it, but that's a gamble: You might get a Featured Snippet with the release date, or you might get a half-dozen links to articles that may or may not have the information, and then you have to read.

The information's out there, but it's buried behind a lot of clicks and forms. Why isn't there just a calendar, like my Google calendar, that just shows you what's coming out and what's recently come out?

Now there is!

Apiary

API lists are long. Life is short.

Built over roughly a week with jQuery, Apiary serves as a UI layer for a well-maintained API of information about other APIs. It makes it possible to quickly and easily sort through a list of ~700 APIs, filtering them by content and features.

Three Lies and the Truth

A quick quiz about the ridiculousness of reality.

Is macaroni named after a person? Did Ben Franklin invent the charcoal grill? Is breakfast really the most important meal of the day? This quiz, built in a couple of days, makes use of randomization and short play sessions to encourage replay and diversity of player experience.
